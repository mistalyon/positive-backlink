Frequently asked

How do I add my first site?

Sign in, go to Dashboard › Sites tab, click Add Site, paste your URL, and select your niche. Verification takes under 60 seconds.

How long does an exchange take?

Match-to-published averages 18 hours. AI Watchdog then monitors the new link every 12 hours.

What is the refund policy?

Credits are non-refundable but never expire. Subscription plans can be cancelled anytime; you keep access until the end of the billing period.

Why was my site rejected?

Common reasons: DR below 10, fewer than 5 indexed pages, adult/gambling/PBN content, or no clear niche signal in the homepage HTML.

How does the ABC method differ from a direct exchange?

In direct exchange, Site A links to Site B and B links back to A. Google can spot this pattern. In the ABC method, A links to B, B links to C, and C links to A — no reciprocal footprint.

What triggers AI Watchdog alerts?

Link removed, link goes 404, link becomes nofollow, anchor text changed, host page becomes noindex, or host page response time exceeds 10s.
