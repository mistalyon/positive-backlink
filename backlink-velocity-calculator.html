Tell us your site age and current backlink profile, and we will calculate the safe weekly RD acquisition rate that keeps you under Google's pattern-detection thresholds.

The formula

We use a piecewise function based on site age tiers, then apply a multiplier for current RD authority and a discount for niche risk. The exact math:

baseSafe = ageMultiplier(months) * (1 + log10(currentRDs + 1) * 0.3)
adjusted = baseSafe * nicheRiskFactor
