How They Differ

Pricing model

PositiveBacklink uses a credit-based system: each successful exchange consumes credits, which you replenish via subscription or top-up. Linkable charges per placement and typically requires a discovery call, with quotes that scale based on publisher DA and niche.

Method

Our ABC Triangular method means Site A links to Site B, Site B links to Site C, and Site C links to Site A — this breaks the reciprocal footprint that Google's algorithms can flag. Linkable does direct outreach to third-party publishers, which produces a different footprint pattern but at a higher per-link cost.

Monitoring

The AI Watchdog re-scans every placed backlink every 12 hours, flagging removals, nofollow flips, or unexpected redirects. Linkable provides quarterly status reports as part of their managed service.

Which Should You Choose?

If you operate a SaaS, run an agency portfolio, or want to scale white-hat link building without per-link contracts, PositiveBacklink is the lower-friction choice. The 10 free credits let you test the matching engine before committing to a plan.

If you prefer to outsource link acquisition entirely, value human curation, and have budget for $300+/link placements, Linkable's managed service is a legitimate option.
