What to evaluate in a backlink platform

Whichever way you compare, five factors actually matter:

  • Method — exchanges, outreach, sponsored articles, and PBNs all have different footprints and risk profiles
  • Niche relevance — a DA-80 link from an unrelated site is worth less than a DA-30 link from a perfect-niche site
  • Monitoring — without automated link checks, you do not know when a placement is removed
  • Cost per link — marginal cost matters more than sticker price once you scale
  • Time to first link — some services take weeks, exchanges can take hours
