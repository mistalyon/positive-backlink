FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is link exchanging safe for SEO in 2026?
Yes, when done with the ABC triangular methodology. We engineered our matching algorithm to prevent footprint detection by Google, ensuring 100% natural-looking link patterns.
What happens if a partner removes my link?
Our AI Watchdog catches it within 12 hours. Your credit is automatically refunded and the partner gets a trust score penalty. Repeat offenders are banned.
How is this different from PBNs or paid links?
Zero money changes hands. Every link is a value-for-value editorial exchange between real, verified sites in matching niches. It is the cleanest white-hat method available.
Do you support no-follow links?
No. We only count do-follow links. Our scanner rejects any partner who switches their link to no-follow, sponsored, or UGC.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your dashboard with one click. No questions asked. Your existing links remain active until renewal period ends.
What niches are supported?
Over 40 categories from Tech and Health to Finance, Travel, Lifestyle, B2B SaaS, eCommerce and more. New categories are added monthly based on demand.
