FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is link exchange against Google guidelines?

Direct reciprocal link schemes are. Our ABC triangular method (A→B, B→C, C→A) and niche-relevant matching create natural-looking link profiles that align with editorial guidelines.

Are the case study results real?

Yes. All charts are from real Search Console data of our customers, shared with permission. Results vary based on niche, content quality, and existing authority.

How does AI Watchdog work?

Every 12 hours we scan all your incoming exchanged links, check rel attributes, anchor text and HTTP status. If a link is removed, we automatically refund your credit.

What is a credit?

Credit is the unit of exchange. You earn 1 credit when you give a link to another site, and spend 1 credit when you receive one. You start with 5 free credits at signup.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no questions asked. Your existing links stay live.
