Link Velocity

Link velocity is the rate at which a website acquires new backlinks over a defined time window, usually expressed as new referring domains per week or per month.

Formula

Velocity = (RDs this period) - (RDs previous period)

Why it matters

Google's spam systems look for statistical anomalies in link growth relative to a site's historical baseline. A sudden spike from a low baseline is a classic footprint of link-buying schemes and can trigger algorithmic demotions or manual reviews.

Safe ranges

Site ageSafe RDs / week
0-3 months1-3
3-12 months3-8
1-3 years5-15
3+ years10-30
