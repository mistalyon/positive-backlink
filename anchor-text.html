Anchor Text

Anchor text is the visible, clickable text of a hyperlink. Google has used anchor signals since the original PageRank paper to understand what the destination page is about.

The five types

TypeExampleSafe %
BrandedPositiveBacklink35-50%
Naked URLhttps://example.com15-25%
Genericclick here10-20%
Partial matchSEO exchange platform10-15%
Exact matchbuy backlinks1-5%

Why it matters

An unbalanced anchor profile, especially one with too many exact-match anchors, is the leading cause of Penguin-style over-optimization penalties. A natural profile is the strongest defense.
