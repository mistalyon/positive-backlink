// ABOUT

Ethical link building, automated.

PositiveBacklink exists because every credible website deserves the SEO traction that enterprise teams take for granted — without buying links, joining shady PBNs, or burning weeks on cold outreach.

Why we built this

Link building in 2026 is broken in two opposite ways. On one side, paid backlink marketplaces still exist — link farms with a UI — and Google keeps refining its algorithms to neutralize them. On the other side, manual outreach is technically white-hat but takes 3-5 hours per landed link, putting it out of reach for small teams and solo founders.

We watched dozens of indie SaaS founders, e-commerce stores, and local service businesses publish genuinely useful content — and then sit at page 4 of Google forever because nobody could find them to link.

PositiveBacklink solves the discovery and matching problem. You list your site once, get matched with niche-relevant partners, and exchange contextual links through the ABC triangular method — not the obvious A↔B reciprocal pattern that algorithms flag.

The ABC method, in plain terms

Traditional link exchange: Site A links to Site B. Site B links back to Site A. Easy to detect, easy to discount.

ABC triangular exchange: Site A links to Site B. Site B links to Site C. Site C links to Site A. No reciprocal footprint, every link looks editorial, and — most importantly — each link only happens between sites that are genuinely topically related.

That last part is what makes it white-hat. We score niche relevance on every potential pairing before suggesting an exchange. A SaaS marketing blog linking to a SaaS tool? Yes. A pet store linking to a JavaScript framework? Never.

Our four principles

Relevance over volume

One link from a topically aligned site beats ten from random domains. We optimize for fit, not count.

Transparency by default

You see every match, every partner site, every anchor text before anything goes live. No black-box recommendations.

Continuous monitoring

The AI Watchdog re-checks every exchanged link every 12 hours. If a partner removes your link, we know within hours, not weeks.

Editorial quality bar

Every member site is reviewed for content quality, real audience signals, and a clean backlink profile before joining the pool.

What we don’t do

  • We don’t sell links. Paid backlinks violate Google’s guidelines and we won’t put your site at risk.
  • We don’t run PBNs. No private blog networks, no expired-domain farms, no AI-generated host sites.
  • We don’t hide partners. You always know which site you’re exchanging with before committing.
  • We don’t use shady anchors. Anchor diversity is enforced platform-wide — read more in our anchor text diversity playbook.
