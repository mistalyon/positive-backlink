About PositiveBacklink

We exist because link building should not require a six-figure agency contract or a year of unanswered cold emails. PositiveBacklink turns earned backlinks into a programmable utility — safe, fast, and verifiable.

The problem we solve

Most link-building services in 2026 fall into one of three buckets:

  1. Black-hat PBNs that rank for 90 days then collapse
  2. Manual outreach agencies at $150-500 per link with 8-week timelines
  3. Marketplaces that sell links with no quality control or post-publish monitoring

PositiveBacklink is none of these. We are a software-driven exchange where every match is niche-relevant, every link is monitored every 12 hours by AI Watchdog, and the ABC triangular method eliminates the reciprocal footprint that gets reciprocal exchanges penalized.

Our principles

1. White-hat or nothing

We refuse PBN inventory, paid links disguised as editorial, and any tactic that violates Google's spam policies. If it would not pass a manual review, it does not enter our exchange.

2. Transparent pricing

One subscription, one credit price. No setup fees, no per-link surcharges, no "premium domains" upsell. The price you see is the price you pay.

3. Earned, not bought

Every link in our network is earned through reciprocal value exchange. Members give links to receive links. This keeps the network self-cleaning: low-quality sites cannot rent their way in.
