Frequently asked questions

Is there a free plan?
Yes. The Starter plan is free forever and includes 1 site, 5 monthly exchange credits, and 12-hour Watchdog checks. No credit card required.
What is a credit?
One credit equals one confirmed backlink exchange. Credits refresh every month. On paid plans, unused credits roll over (30 days on Growth, 60 days on Scale).
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Plans are month-to-month with no contracts. Annual billing offers 20% off but is fully refundable in the first 14 days.
Do you offer custom enterprise pricing?
Yes. For agencies managing 20+ client sites or publishers with 500+ monthly exchange needs, contact us for custom Scale+ pricing with volume discounts.
What payment methods are accepted?
All major credit cards via Stripe. Annual plans can also pay by invoice and wire transfer (USD or EUR).
Is there a money-back guarantee?
Yes. 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans, no questions asked. Cancel from your dashboard and we refund automatically.
Do unused credits expire?
On the Starter plan, credits reset each month. On Growth, unused credits roll over for 30 days. On Scale, 60 days.
Can I upgrade or downgrade?
Yes, at any time. Upgrades are prorated immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
