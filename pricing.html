Frequently asked questions

What are credits and how are they used?

Credits are how exchanges are priced on the platform. When you place a link on another site, you earn credits. When you place a link on your site through the network, you spend credits. Typical exchange: 5-15 credits depending on DR.

Do credits expire?

No. One-time credit packs never expire. Monthly plan credits do not roll over to the next month but you can buy top-up packs anytime.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, anytime from your dashboard. Your account stays Pro/Agency until the end of the billing period, then drops to Starter. Existing credits stay in your account.

What happens if a link is removed?

AI Watchdog detects removal within 12 hours and auto-refunds your credits. The offending site loses tier points.

Is there a free trial?

Pro plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required for Starter. Cancel before the trial ends to pay nothing.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, 14-day money-back guarantee on subscriptions. Credit packs are refundable if unused within 7 days of purchase.
