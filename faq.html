Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about ABC triangular link building, AI Watchdog monitoring, credits, pricing, and how PositiveBacklink keeps your link profile safe.

General

What is PositiveBacklink?

PositiveBacklink is an automated white-hat backlink exchange platform. It matches your site with niche-relevant partners using AI scoring, places contextual links via the ABC triangular method, and monitors them 24/7 with the AI Watchdog so links never silently disappear.

Who is PositiveBacklink for?

It is built for SaaS founders, agencies, affiliate marketers, and content site owners who need real editorial backlinks without manual outreach, private blog networks, or PBN risk.

How is this different from a traditional link exchange?

Traditional A↔B exchanges create reciprocal footprints that Google flags. PositiveBacklink uses ABC triangular linking (Site A links to B, B links to C, C links to A) so each site receives a clean, non-reciprocal editorial link from a niche-relevant page.

Do I need to write content?

No. Partner sites publish editorial mentions on existing or new posts that match your niche. You approve placements before they go live.

What languages are supported?

English, Spanish, German, French, Turkish, and Portuguese partner pools are active. Additional locales open as partner density grows.

ABC Method

What is ABC triangular link building?

It is a 3-site rotation where Site A links to Site B, Site B links to Site C, and Site C links to Site A. No two sites link back to each other directly, eliminating the reciprocal pattern Google's link spam algorithms detect.

Is the ABC method safe from Google penalties?

When combined with niche relevance, anchor diversity, and natural link velocity, ABC linking produces patterns indistinguishable from organic editorial linking. PositiveBacklink enforces all three guardrails automatically.

How are ABC triangles assembled?

Our matching engine scores every potential partner on niche relevance (semantic embedding), Domain Authority band, language, geo, and current link profile. It then assembles 3-site rotations where all three sites pass relevance and DA thresholds.

Can I see who I'm linking to before publish?

Yes. Every triangle proposal shows the partner URLs, anchor candidates, target page, and niche relevance score. You approve or reject before any link goes live.

What if a partner removes my link later?

The AI Watchdog rechecks every placement every 12 hours. If a link drops, becomes nofollow, or the page returns 404, you are notified, the partner is flagged, and your credits are restored automatically.

Pricing & Credits

How does the credit system work?

One credit equals one approved outbound link placement. Credits are consumed when your site links out to a partner. Inbound links to your site are free; credits only meter your outgoing contribution to the exchange.

Is there a free tier?

Yes. The free tier includes 5 placements per month, AI Watchdog monitoring, and access to all free SEO tools. No credit card required.

Do unused credits roll over?

On paid plans, up to one month of unused credits roll forward. This prevents punishment for slow months while discouraging stockpiling.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your dashboard in one click. Active triangles continue to honor monitoring through the current billing cycle.

Do you offer agency or white-label pricing?

Yes. Agency plans include multi-site dashboards, client-scoped reporting, and team seats. Contact sales for white-label options.

AI Watchdog & Technical

What does the AI Watchdog actually check?

Every 12 hours it verifies the link still exists, anchor text matches what was approved, rel attribute (dofollow vs nofollow) is unchanged, the target page returns 200, the link is not buried below visible content, and the surrounding paragraph still matches the agreed topic.

How are niche relevance scores calculated?

We use semantic embeddings of your site's top-ranking pages versus the partner's content, weighted by topical authority signals and entity overlap. Scores below 70/100 are auto-rejected from triangle assembly.

Do you support staging or sandbox sites?

Yes. You can connect staging environments with HTTP basic auth or a verification meta tag. Live linking only activates on production domains you verify ownership of.

How do you verify domain ownership?

Three methods: DNS TXT record, HTML meta tag, or upload of a verification file to /well-known/. You only need one to pass.

Is there an API?

Yes. The REST API lets you list triangles, fetch monitoring reports, manage credits, and webhook on link status changes. API access is included on Pro and Agency plans.

Where is data stored?

All data is stored in EU-based Supabase Postgres with row-level security. We are GDPR-compliant and never sell user data.

Safety & Compliance

Will Google penalize me for using PositiveBacklink?

No. The ABC method, niche relevance enforcement, and natural anchor distribution match the patterns of fully organic link building. We follow Google's E-E-A-T and link spam guidelines strictly.

Do you allow link velocity controls?

Yes. You set a maximum new placements per week per domain. The engine respects this cap and spaces deployments naturally rather than dumping links in bursts.

What happens if a partner is later flagged as toxic?

If a partner's domain trust drops or is later identified as part of a link scheme, the AI Watchdog suggests a disavow entry and offers a one-click replacement triangle. Your credits are not consumed for replaced placements.

Are paid links allowed?

No. PositiveBacklink only facilitates editorial exchange. We do not allow money to change hands between partners for individual links. Subscription fees pay for the platform, monitoring, and matching engine — not for the links themselves.

Can I exclude specific competitors or niches?

Yes. Your dashboard has a blocklist for competitor domains and niche keywords. The matcher never proposes blocked partners.

Do you accept gambling, adult, or pharma sites?

No. We do not onboard YMYL-restricted verticals, adult content, gambling, unregulated pharma, or any niche that increases penalty risk for clean partners.

What if I want to delete my account?

Self-serve account deletion is available from your dashboard. All linked partners are notified, active triangles are gracefully retired, and your data is purged within 30 days per GDPR.
