Anchor Text Analyzer

Paste one anchor text per line. We classify each into 5 categories and show you the distribution. Everything runs in your browser; nothing is sent to our servers.

Results will appear here.

How the classifier works

  • Branded: Anchor contains your brand name (case insensitive)
  • Naked URL: Anchor contains your domain or starts with http
  • Generic: Anchor matches a list of common generic phrases (click here, read more, this site, etc.)
  • Exact match: Anchor is a money keyword pattern (2-4 words, no brand, no generic)
  • Partial match: Everything else

Safe distribution targets

Aim for 35-50% branded, 15-25% naked, 10-20% generic, 10-15% partial, and 1-5% exact. See our anchor distribution playbook for the full methodology.
