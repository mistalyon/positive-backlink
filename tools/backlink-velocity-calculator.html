How the Velocity Calculation Works

The tool computes required monthly velocity = (target - current) / months, then compares this required pace against your historical baseline. Search engines analyze growth patterns over time, and a sudden jump above your baseline can be interpreted as unnatural acquisition.

The risk thresholds: under 30% spike = SAFE, 30-100% spike = CAUTION, over 100% spike = HIGH RISK. Pairs well with our link velocity glossary entry.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is backlink velocity?
Backlink velocity is the rate at which a site acquires new backlinks over time, usually measured as links gained per month.
What velocity is considered safe?
A growth rate within 10-25% of your current monthly average is generally safe.
Is this tool free?
Yes, completely free. All calculations run in your browser; nothing is sent to a server.
How accurate is the risk flag?
The risk flag uses publicly documented thresholds. It is a heuristic guide, not a guarantee.
